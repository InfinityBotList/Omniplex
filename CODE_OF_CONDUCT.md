# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to providing a welcoming and inspiring community for all. This Code of Conduct outlines our expectations for all those who participate in our community, as well as the consequences for unacceptable behavior.

We invite all those who participate in Omniplex to help us create safe and positive experiences for everyone.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to creating a positive environment include:

- **Being respectful** - Using welcoming and inclusive language
- **Being appreciative** - Acknowledging and appreciating the contributions of others
- **Being understanding** - Accepting constructive criticism gracefully
- **Being collaborative** - Working together toward common goals
- **Being empathetic** - Considering the feelings and perspectives of others
- **Being professional** - Keeping discussions on topic and relevant

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- Harassment or intimidation of any kind
- Discrimination based on race, gender, sexual orientation, disability, or other protected characteristics
- Trolling, insulting, or derogatory comments
- Public or private harassment or threats
- Publishing others' private information without consent
- Spam or promotional content
- Any conduct that would be considered inappropriate in a professional setting

## Reporting and Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported by contacting the project team at conduct@omniplex.gg. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.

Project maintainers are obligated to:

- Maintain the confidentiality of the reporter
- Treat all reports seriously and investigate thoroughly
- Take appropriate action based on the severity of the violation
- Provide feedback to the reporter when possible

## Consequences

Community members who violate this Code of Conduct may face:

- **Warning** - For minor violations, a private warning and explanation of why the behavior was inappropriate
- **Temporary Ban** - For repeated or moderate violations, temporary removal from community spaces
- **Permanent Ban** - For severe or repeated violations after warnings, permanent removal from the community

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to:

- All official Omniplex community spaces (GitHub, Discord, forums, etc.)
- Interactions between community members online and offline related to the project
- Official events and conferences

## Appeals

Community members who have been banned may appeal the decision by submacting a written appeal to conduct@omniplex.gg. Appeals will be reviewed by the project leadership and a decision will be made within 7 days.
